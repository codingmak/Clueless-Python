SUGGESTION

Able to click Suggest button.

Suggest button sends packet to server for a request to pick a player.

Server returns another packet  allowing the client to pick a player.  That takes one turn for client to suggest.

Blank quadrant displays/blits player that was chosen by the client and it cannot be yourself.
